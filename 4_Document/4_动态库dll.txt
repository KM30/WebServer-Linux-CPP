制作动态库
->gcc -c -fpic add.c div.c mult.c sub.c
->gcc -shared add.o div.o mult.o sub.o -o libcalc.so

使用动态库
->gcc main.c -o main -I ./include/ -L ./lib/ -l calc

	添加动态库文件的绝对路径，到动态载入器环境变量里<-
	1、(仅限当前终端可用)
	->ldd
	->pwd
	->export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=$LD_LIBRARY_PATH:/home/zy/Documents/3Linux_cpp/2_lib_os/library/lib
	->env

	2、(用户级别)
	->vim .bashrc
	export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=$LD_LIBRARY_PATH:/home/zy/Documents/3Linux_cpp/2_lib_os/library/lib
	->source .bashrc

	3、(系统级别)
	-> sudo vim /etc/profile
	export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=$LD_LIBRARY_PATH:/home/zy/Documents/3Linux_cpp/2_lib_os/library/lib
	->source /etc/profile

	4、(配置文件级别)
	->sudo vim /etc/ld.so.conf
	export LD_LIBRARY_PATH=$LD_LIBRARY_PATH:/home/zy/Documents/3Linux_cpp/2_lib_os/library/lib
	->sudo ldconfig
